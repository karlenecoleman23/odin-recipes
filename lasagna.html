World's Best Lasagna

Welcome to the recipe for the world's best lasagna! I'm so glad you've decided to make this delicious food for your family. Your kids will be asking for seconds in no time!

Ingredients

  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Ground Beef
  • Uncooked Noodles
  • White Onion
  • Tomato Paste
  • Crushed Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Brown ground beef. Drain and set aside.
  3. Boil noodles.
  4. Mix ricotta, onion, tomato paste, ground beef and crushed tomatoes together.
  5. Layer cooked noodles along the bottom of your dish and then add ricotta mixture.
  6. Repeat until you've reached the top of your dish.
  7. Cook for 40 minutes on 400 degrees in your over.
