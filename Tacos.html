World's Best Tacos

Welcome to the recipe for the world's best tacos! I'm so glad you've decided to make this delicious food for your family. Your kids will be asking for seconds in no time!

Ingredients

  • Taco Shells
  • Ground Beef
  • Tomatoes
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Condiments

Steps

  1. Brown ground beef and add taco seasoning.
  2. Add ground beef to buns.
  3. Chop vegetables.
  4. Assemble tacos.
